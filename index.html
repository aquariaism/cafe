Our Story

Roasted with patience, served with soul.

Brew Haven started in 2015 as a single roasting drum in a garage and a stubborn belief that coffee is worth doing properly. Today we source directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia and Sumatra, roast in-house every week, and build our kitchen menu around the same standard: honest ingredients, unhurried technique.

Every cup, plate and pastry that leaves our counter carries that same intention — this is a place to slow down, not just grab and go.

  • Direct-Trade BeansSourced from farms we've visited ourselves
  • In-House RoastingSmall batches roasted fresh weekly
  • Made With CareEvery dish plated to order, never rushed
